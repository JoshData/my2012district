What's the purpose of this tool?

Due to redistricting, many congressional districts have changed for 2012, which could mean you will be voting in a different district than before.

This tool gives you an easy way to get the low down on your current congressional district and its candidates. Simply enter your address and click "Show my district", and it will show you your current district, its candidates, and what's changed (if anything) with the redistricting.
